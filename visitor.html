More information about Stonehaven!

Stonehaven Open Air Pool: If you visit during the warmer months, the Stonehaven Open Air Pool is a unique attraction. It's one of the few remaining outdoor saltwater pools in the UK and provides a refreshing experience.
Opening times as follows: Monday - Friday (1pm - 7:30pm), Saturday - Sunday (10am - 6pm).

Dunnottar Castle: A stunning cliff-top fortress that offers breathtaking views of the North Sea. The castle has a rich history and is known for its dramatic location.

Stonehaven Harbour: The charming harbor area is a great place for a leisurely stroll. It's lined with shops, restaurants, and cafes, offering a delightful atmosphere.

St. Cyrus National Nature Reserve: Located nearby, this nature reserve offers a beautiful sandy beach and diverse wildlife. It's a great place for nature walks and birdwatching.
